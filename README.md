# 📊 Store Sales Dashboard | Power BI

# 📌 Project Overview

The Store Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to monitor and analyze sales performance across different regions, customer segments, product categories, shipping methods, and payment modes.

The dashboard provides business users with clear visual insights into sales, profit, quantity sold, and delivery performance, enabling data-driven decision-making.

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data every day, making it difficult to identify performance trends and business opportunities.

This dashboard answers important business questions such as:

- Which region generates the highest sales?
- Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
- Which sub-categories are top-performing?
- Which payment methods are preferred by customers?
- Which shipping modes generate the highest sales?
- How do monthly sales and profits change over time?

- Track monthly sales and profit trends.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

### Sales Performance

- Total sales reached **2 Million**.
- Total profit generated was **175K**.
- More than **22K products** were sold.

### Regional Performance

- The **West** region generated the highest sales.
- The **South** region recorded the lowest contribution.

### Customer Segments

- **Consumer** customers contributed the largest share of sales.
- Corporate and Home Office segments followed.

### Product Performance

- **Office Supplies** generated the highest sales.
- **Phones** were the top-selling sub-category.
- Chairs and Binders also contributed significantly.

### Payment Analysis

- **Cash on Delivery (COD)** was the most frequently used payment method.
- Online and Card payments followed.

### Shipping Analysis

- **Standard Class** shipping generated the highest sales.
- Same Day delivery represented the smallest share.

### Sales Trend

- Sales increased steadily toward the end of the year.
- November and December recorded the strongest sales performance.

# 📌 Business Recommendations

Based on dashboard insights:

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
- Increase inventory for top-selling products like Phones and Chairs.
- Promote faster shipping options where feasible.
- Encourage digital payment adoption through discounts or rewards.
- Expand successful sales strategies to lower-performing regions.
- Monitor seasonal demand to improve inventory planning.
